About Us

A group of young and passionate programmers.

We love code.

Working computers is our specialty. We don't focus on what others think of our code. In fact, we don't take reviews for anything. The only thing we get out of our reviews are good ideas.

How we do it.

We work hard on what we make, and all programs have to pass rigorous testing before full release to the public. That is why it takes so long to release. We do this in the hope that people will want to use it more if they know how much effort it takes to make something beautiful. By hand-picking our members, we ensure that they are serious about what they want to do, and that they will always make reliable programs. We wrote this in the hope that you will see how hard we work and try one of our programs.

What happened to A4A?

CoreTech is now A4A. This means that everything is being re-branded. Because of that, we will no longer be offering software created by A4A to the public. But don't worry, we are hard at work creating better programs to replace those older ones. These apps should be out around June or July. We apologise for the wait, but we want to deliver only the best. If you already own A4A software, you will be able to use it as normal.
